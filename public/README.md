## 🛠️ What We Do

### 🔐 Cybersecurity
We offer comprehensive security services to help organizations identify, assess, and mitigate digital threats:
- **Penetration Testing** — Web, network, mobile, and API-level intrusion simulations
- **Vulnerability Assessments** — Systematic identification and prioritization of security weaknesses
- **Information Security Compliance** — ISO 27001, GDPR, and industry-standard compliance frameworks
- **Security Audits & Risk Assessments** — In-depth analysis of organizational security posture
- **Incident Response Consulting** — Guidance and support during and after security incidents

### 🌐 Full-Stack Web Development
We design and build robust, production-ready web applications using modern stacks:
- **Frontend:** React, Next.js, Vite, TypeScript, JavaScript

## 🚀 Flagship Projects

### 📄 ContractIQ
> *Intelligent Contract Lifecycle Management*

A full-featured **contract management system** built for modern organizations. ContractIQ redefines how teams create, negotiate, and manage contracts through:
- **Bilateral Collaboration** — Real-time multi-party contract negotiation and review workflows
- **Custom Workflow Engine** — Fully customizable contract procedures, approval chains, and automation rules
- **Contract Lifecycle Management** — End-to-end tracking from drafting to execution and renewal
- **Legal Operations Ready** — Designed to align with legal teams' needs while being accessible to non-legal stakeholders

---

### 🧠 SereneMind
> *Your Daily Mental Wellness Companion*

A mental health application built with a mission to support the mental wellness journey of **Kenyan youth and young people across society**. SereneMind provides:
- **AI Companion** — An empathetic, conversational AI for daily emotional check-ins and support
- **Journaling** — Private, structured journaling with mood tracking and reflection prompts
- **Community Spaces** — Safe peer-support communities moderated for positive engagement
- **Therapy Finder** — A directory to connect users with licensed therapists and counselors in Kenya
- **Mental Health Resources** — Curated articles, exercises, and guided wellness content

---

### ⚖️ Legal Management System
> *Streamlining Legal Operations*

A comprehensive legal operations platform designed to help law firms and legal departments manage cases, documentation, client relationships, and compliance workflows efficiently.
